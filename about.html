About

Hello, my name is Honey! I have always loved making wonderful things with paper, and that's what encouraged me to make this website. Here, you can find an extensive list of various origami designs.

Origami is the art of paper folding, often associated with Japanese culture. The goal is to transform a flat square sheet of paper into a finished sculpture through folding and sculpting techniques. The small number of basic origami folds can be combined in a variety of ways to make intricate designs. The best-known origami model is the Japanese paper crane.
